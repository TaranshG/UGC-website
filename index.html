FAQ

Common questions

Do you work with brands outside Canada?

Yes. I work remotely and can receive products by mail when needed for physical demos.

Do you appear on camera?

Yes. I can do on-camera videos, voiceover-only videos, or product B-roll depending on what works for your campaign.

Can you create content in Hindi or French?

Yes. English and Hindi are both fluent. French is available at an intermediate level for simple product content.

What types of products are the best fit?

Apps, tech products, student tools, productivity software, lifestyle products, skincare and grooming, gadgets, and everyday consumer products.

Can I provide a script or talking points?

Yes. I can work from your talking points or a full script. I'll usually adjust the wording slightly to make it sound natural for short-form video.

What should I include when I reach out?

The product or service, the campaign goal, deliverables needed, how you plan to use the content, your timeline, and any style references you like.
